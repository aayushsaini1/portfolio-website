# Case Study: Redefining Fleet Tech UX

An end-to-end UX study of mobile and desktop interfaces designed for dispatchers, drivers, and logistics operators.

## 01. The Problem Space
Logistics operators manage hundreds of vehicles simultaneously under high stress. Default legacy systems were slow, cluttered, and prone to user input errors.

## 02. Redesign Process
- **Field research:** Riding with drivers to understand real-world context and lighting conditions.
- **Dark UI mode:** Prioritized to minimize eye strain during night shifts.
- **Tap target adjustments:** Large, accessible controls optimized for in-vehicle dashboards.

## 03. Outcome
- Driver dispatch errors dropped by **30%**.
- Increased daily route optimization efficiency by **15%**.
